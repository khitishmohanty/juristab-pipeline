The average consumer
Whether a commercial practice breaches the requirements of professional diligence
or the prohibitions of misleading and aggressive practices will be judged by reference
(as appropriate) to:
the ‘average consumer’
the ‘average member’ of a targeted group of consumers
the ‘average member’ of a vulnerable group of consumers.[footnote 29]
